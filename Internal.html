God of War

God of War is an action-advententure game franchise created by David Jaffe at Sony's Santa Monica studio. It began in 2005 on the PlayStation 2 (PS2) video game console and has become a falgship series for PlayStation.
Based on ancient mythologies, the story follows Kratos, a Spartan warrior and later the Greek God of War, who was tricked into killing his family by his former master. the original Greek God of War. The Greek-based games see Kratos follow thw path of vengance due to the machinations if the Olympian gods, while the Norse-based games, which introduce his son Atreus as a second protagonist, show Kratos on a path of redemption and inadvertently coming into conflict with the Norse gods.
